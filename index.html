Why Fynancer?

01. goals

Fynancer is built for every Singaporeans, young and old, with the aim to educate user's financial habits.

02. What makes us different

Our system offers appealing dashboards with an easy to use interface that can be used by everyone, even those who does not have prior excel knowledge.

Assets & Liabilities

Apart from basic input system of assets and liabilities, we have investment portfolio that is updated every 1 minute as well as many other cool dashboards.

Export to PDF

Forecasts

With built in forecast analytics, you can now view the predicted assets and liabilities for the next year! Fynancer will also tell you your status based on this year's data.
